Multi-Deck System

You can create multiple vocabulary decks to organize your learning:

  • Deck 1: Main vocabulary
  • Deck 2: HSK 1 words
  • Deck 3: Business Chinese
  • And so on...

Use the deck switcher bar at the top of the page to switch between decks.

üì• Step 3: Export to Anki

Once you've added words, export them to Anki to start studying:

Export Steps:

  1. Go to your Dictionary page
  2. Click the "Export CSV" button
  3. Save the .csv file to your device

Import to Anki:

  1. Open Anki on your device
  2. Tap/Click "Import" or open the CSV file
  3. Set Field separator to "Comma"
  4. Enable "Allow HTML in fields"
  5. Choose a Note Type (e.g., "Basic")
  6. Choose or create a Deck (e.g., "Personal Vocabulary")
  7. Click "Import" üéâ
üí° Important: Make sure to enable "Allow HTML in fields" to see colored pinyin and formatted examples!

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use this on multiple devices?

Yes! Your vocabulary is stored in the cloud. Just log in from any device to access your decks.

Q: How many words can I add?

Unlimited! Add as many words as you need across multiple decks.

Q: What data is included for each word?

Each word includes: Chinese character, colored pinyin, translation, example sentences, stroke order GIFs, pronunciation audio, and an exemplary image.

Q: Can I delete words?

Yes, go to your Dictionary page and click the Delete button next to any word.

Q: Is my data safe?

Yes, your data is stored securely in Supabase with encrypted connections.
